About Us

Caring with Integrity Home Care was founded from the owner’s deep passion for helping others and their personal experience with providing care. Growing up, she always found joy in caring for people and supporting those in need. This became even more personal when her father required care and experience that taught her firsthand how vital compassionate, and reliable assistance is for families facing similar challenges. She understands the emotional and physical toll it can take when a loved one is in need, which drives her commitment to providing high-quality, personalized care.

Mission

Caring With Integrity Home Care's mission is to empower clients to maintain their independence and dignity while offering peace of mind to their families. At Caring with Integrity Home Care, we strive to create a supportive environment where every client feels valued, respected and genuinely cared for. This commitment to excellence and personalized attention drives everything we do.

Private Pay

For individuals seeking personalized and flexible home care solutions in Philadelphia, our Private Pay services provide an excellent option. With no restrictions from insurance plans or eligibility requirements, you have the freedom to design a care plan that fits your unique needs and preferences. Whether you require companionship, assistance with daily activities, or specialized care, our professional caregivers deliver compassionate support tailored to your schedule. Private Pay ensures immediate access to quality care and the ability to adapt services as your circumstances change, providing peace of mind for you and your loved ones.
